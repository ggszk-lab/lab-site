**Degree**
Ph.D. in Engineering

**Biography**
Gengo Suzuki received his M.Sc. in Mathematics from Tohoku University in 1990. He joined Nippon Telegraph and Telephone Corporation (NTT) the same year, where he was involved in research and development on network management database construction, data analysis and visualization, database design, multi-database systems, XML data management, graph data management, requirements engineering, and common frameworks. He received his Ph.D. in Engineering from the University of Electro-Communications in 2013. He served as a part-time lecturer at the University of Electro-Communications (2013) and Kogakuin University (2018--2019). He has been at his current position since April 2020.

**Professional Memberships**
Information Processing Society of Japan (IPSJ), Institute of Electronics, Information and Communication Engineers (IEICE), the Database Society of Japan (DBSJ), Japan Social Data Science Society (JSDSS), Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)
